About me

I am entering into my senior year at Texas A&M and plan to graduate in May 2023 with a two degrees: computer science (BS) with a minor in cybersecurity and applied mathematics (BS) with a minor in statistics. I was born and raised an Aggie and I truly love to learn.

I value teamwork and prefer to work in an environment that facilitates constant communication and cross-functional involvement. I seek to listen to ideas, make compromises, and follow through on design and development efforts.

In my free time I enjoy cooking, artisan coffee, and photography. I am passionate about people and I have found a way to serve others through a local organization called Impact Retreat. It is a way that I can help connect incoming Texas A&M and Blinn students each year to a local community while also growing my leadership skills.
